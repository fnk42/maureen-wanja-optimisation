Activity & Content

Even one original post per month builds visibility and personal authority.
Quick win
Current
Activity level unknown. Most EA candidates do not post — which means one original post immediately distinguishes a profile.
→
Recommended

One original post per month. Topics that perform well for EA positioning:

  • What makes executive-assistant relationships work well — from the EA's perspective
  • A tool or workflow that has improved your efficiency (Outlook calendar management, filing systems)
  • An observation about the EA role in a fast-paced operational environment
  • A short reflection on a professional lesson from your career

Posts of 150–250 words with no external links consistently outperform link-shares on LinkedIn.

EA candidates rarely post on LinkedIn — which is precisely why one original post per month creates outsized visibility. Recruiters who see an EA candidate sharing a thoughtful observation about executive support immediately categorise them as more engaged and intentional than the average applicant. One post per month is a sustainable commitment alongside a full-time role.
